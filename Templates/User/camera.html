Improve your balance and concentration with a calm and stable standing posture.

How to do Tree Pose

1

Stand straight with one standing leg.

2

Lift the other foot and place it on the opposite inner calf or thigh.

3

Open the bent knee outward.

4

Bring your hands to prayer or raise them overhead.
